BACKGROUND

VICTORS AFRICA GENERAL TRADING LTD is a South Sudan based company engaged in the supply and installation of energy and digital components. The company is primarily engaged in the business of Electricity, solar installation and transmission components.


The company offers a range of accessories and services for electricity and solar system. Victor's Africa Hi-Tech solar is a division of the company, which is engaged in the business of engineering software solutions. Our geographic segments are Juba and other states of South Sudan. It services diverse clients across the South for electronic and solar components. Its forging customers include various national and international customers.


VISION

To be a recognized customer driven company in the renewable energy and digital migration sector focused towards exceeding customer expectations.


MISSION

To provide energy and digital migration solutions that conserve natural resources and promote individual and community self-reliance and explore the alternatives to the existing energy solutions.
